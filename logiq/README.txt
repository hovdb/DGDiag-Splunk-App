Digital Guardian logiq is an app to ingest and read the Digital Guardian diag logs from the end-point agent.

Requirements:
The app is configured to ingest data from a folder called logiq in the opt directory (/opt/logiq).
DGDiag needs to be extracted into subfolders under /opt/logiq.

The app was developed by Bjorn Hovd Digital Guardian.
